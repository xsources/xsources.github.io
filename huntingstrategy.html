Strategies for success in animals

A hunting sequence of 2 sloughi greyhounds, rich in teachings in a magical setting, with a musical "gnawi" background, in the Algerian sahara. In this sequence it is easy to observe:
  1. Hunting strategy of two sloughi
  2. Optimal management of the expended energy
  3. Successful hunting.

The strategy consists in keeping the prey (sahara bunny) in a state of extreme escape, spending all its energy until exhaustion. All that is needed for the 2 sloughi is to take position one behind the other in the race, so that they relay each other on short phases with maximum speed.

Such phases are so well alternated and regulated that the prey is depleted long before the predators that catch it. It is the eternal prey-predator competition.
